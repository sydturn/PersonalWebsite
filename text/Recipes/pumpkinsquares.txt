<p>Batter<br />1/2 cup butter<br />1/2 cup sugar<br />1/2 brown sugar<br />2 eggs<br />14oz canned pumpkin<br />1 cup flour<br />1 tsp cinnamon<br />1/4 tsp nutmeg<br />1/4 tsp ginger<br />1 tsp baking powder<br />1/2 tsp baking soda<br />1/4 tsp salt</p>
<p>1) cream butter and sugar together<br />2) beat in eggs, one at a time<br />3) slowly beat in pumpkin<br />4) add remaining ingredients and stir<br />5) pour batter into 9x13 cake pan<br />5) bake at 350 degrees for 25 minutes<br />6) cool</p>
<p>Icing<br />125g cream cheese<br />1/4 cup butter<br />1 tsp vanilla<br />2 cups icing sugar</p>
<p>1) beat together cream cheese, butter and vanilla<br />2) a icing sugar one spoonfull at a time while stiring<br />3) apply icing to cake</p>
