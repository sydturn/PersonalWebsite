<p>Cake<br />1 box Devils food cake w/ pudding<br />3 eggs<br />1 tbsp almond flavoring<br />19 oz. cherry pie filling<br />1 cup semisweet chocolate chips</p>
<p>1) Mix all ingredients together with spoon.<br />2) Turn into greased 9x13 inch pan.<br />3) Bake 350 degrees for 45 to 50 minutes or until inserted toothpick comes out clean<br />4) cool and glaze</p>
<p>Glaze<br />1/2 cup semi sweetened chocolate chips<br />1 tbsp Butter or margarine<br />2 tbsp Milk<br />1/2 cup Icing Sugar</p>
<p>1) Heat chips, butter and milk in saucepan over medium heat<br />2) stir in icing sugar<br />3) spread over cooled cake</p>
