<p>Crust<br />1/2 cup margarine or butter<br />3/4 cup brown sugar<br />1 large egg<br />1 tsp vanilla<br />1 1/2 cup flour<br /><br/>Topping<br />1 cup milk chocolate chips<br />1 cup minature marshmellows<br />1/2 cup chopped pecans (I always skip these)<br />300ML sweetened condensed milk (super good for you)<br />chocolate candies (Toppings: I find gummies work AMAZING too, I like to do a mixture)</p>
<p>Crust: <br />1) cream margarine and brown sugar<br />2) beat in egg and vanilla<br />3) add flour<br />4) put onto floured surgace and knead until soft dough forms<br />5) press into 12" pan and form "crust" around the edge</p>
<p>Topping:<br />1) springle chocolate chips, marshmallows and pecans evenly over crust<br />2) Pour condensed milk evenly over top<br />3) bake at 350 degrees for 20 minutes until golden<br />4) immediately place candies on top. Cool completely in pan.</p>
