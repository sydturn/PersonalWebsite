<p>1 cup margarine<br />1 cup packed brown sugar<br />1/2 cup granulated sugar<br />2 eggs<br />1 1/2 tsp vanilla extract<br />2 cups all purpose flour<br />1 tsp baking soda<br />1/2 tsp salt<br />2 cups of semi sweer chocolate chips</p>
<p>1) preheat oven to 375 degrees<br />2) cream margarine and both brown and white sugar together<br />3) add egs and vanilla extract<br />4) stir together glour, baking soda, and salt<br />5) add flour mixture to butter/sugar mixture<br />6) stir in chocolate chips<br />7) bake 10 - 12 minutes</p>
