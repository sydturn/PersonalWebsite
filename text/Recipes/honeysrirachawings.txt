<p>Wings<br />2 tbsp vegetable oil<br />2 lbs wings<br />2 tbsp butter<br />1 tsp garlic powder<br />1 tsp salt<br />1/2 tsp pepper</p>
<p>1) preheat oven to 400 degrees<br />2) coat pan with vegetable oil and dry wings with paper towel<br />3) mix vegetable oil, melted butter, garlic, salt and pepper in a bowl and coat wings<br />4) bake for 45-55 minutes</p>
<p>Sauce<br />5 tbsp butter<br />1/3 cup honey<br />1/4 cup sriracha sauce<br />1 tbs soy sauce<br />2 tsp lime juice</p>
<p>1) melt butter in pot over medium heat<br />2) add honey, sriracha, soy sauce and lime juice and stil until bubbles<br />3) put wings in bowl and mix with sauce<br />4) drizzle honey over wings and serve</p>
