<p>6 tbsp butter<br />6 tbsp flour<br />4 cups milk<br />desired cheese<br />5 cups noodles</p>
<p>1) in a double boiler, melt butter and stir flour into mixture<br />2) add milk and stir until thickened<br />3) once thickened, add cheese and melt into mixture<br />4) add mixture to cooked noodles and bake in over at 250 degrees until desirred texture</p>
<p>**I sometimes like to add cheese to the top before baking too.</p>
