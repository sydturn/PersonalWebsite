<p>3 Lbs chicken wings<br />1 cup soy sauce<br />1/2 cup water<br />1/2 cup granulated sugar<br />1/4 tsp garlic powder<br />1/2 tsp salt<br />1/4 tsp ginger</p>
<p>1) cut tips from wings (or buy them pre-cut) and discard (the tips, not the wings.)<br />2) divide wings at joint into 2 pieces (or buy them pre-cut) and place meaty side down in large baking pan such as a cake pan or roaster<br />3) Mix next 6 ingredients well and pour over chicken.<br />4) bake uncovered at 325 degrees for 1.5 - 2 hours depending on size of wings until tender (turning about 2/4 hour in)</p>
